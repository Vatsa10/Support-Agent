## Frequently Asked Questions

### Billing Questions

#### How do I update my payment method?
You can update your payment method in your account settings. Go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods, then click 'Add' or 'Edit'. We accept all major credit cards and PayPal.

#### What's your refund policy?
We offer full refunds within 30 days of purchase. No questions asked. After 30 days, we provide prorated refunds if applicable.

#### Can I change my subscription plan?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. Upgrades are pro-rated.

### Technical Support

#### My app keeps crashing
We're sorry to hear that! Try these steps:
1. Clear app cache and data
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest app version
4. If issue persists, uninstall and reinstall

#### I forgot my password
No problem! Click 'Forgot Password' on login page. We'll send you reset link via email. Link expires in 24 hours.

#### Is my data secure?
Yes! We use military-grade AES-256 encryption for all data. Regular security audits performed monthly.

### Shipping & Delivery

#### How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping: 5-7 business days
Express shipping: 2-3 business days
Next-day available in select areas

#### Can I track my order?
Yes! You'll receive tracking number via email. Track it on our website or carrier site.

#### What about international shipping?
We ship to 50+ countries. Customs handled automatically. Import taxes may apply.

### Returns & Refunds

#### How do I return an item?
Visit Orders page, select item, click Return. Print label and drop at carrier location.

#### How long for refund to process?
Refunds processed within 5-7 business days after we receive item.

#### Can I exchange instead of refund?
Absolutely! Select 'Exchange' during return process. We'll send replacement immediately.
